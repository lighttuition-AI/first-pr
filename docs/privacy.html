HNL Learning — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Short version: HNL Learning does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. Everything your child or family creates stays on your own device.

Who this is for

HNL Learning is an audio-first learning app for children aged 2–8. We have built it to be safe for children: it asks for no personal details and sends nothing about your child anywhere.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, device identifiers, or analytics. We do not show advertising and we do not use any third-party tracking or advertising services.

Information stored on your device

So the app remembers your child’s progress and any personalisation, the following is saved only on your device and is never uploaded to us or anyone else:

  • Child profiles you create (an age and an avatar choice) and game progress.
  • Any voice clips a grown-up records, and any photos or images a grown-up adds, in the in-app Voiceover Studio and Picture Studio.
  • App settings, such as the chosen visual theme.

This information stays in the app’s private storage on your device. Removing the app deletes it.

Microphone, Photos, and Camera

The app only asks for these when a grown-up actively chooses to use a feature, and whatever is captured stays on the device:

  • Microphone — only when a grown-up records their own voice in the Voiceover Studio. Recordings are saved on the device for use inside the app and are not transmitted anywhere.
  • Photos / Camera — only if a grown-up chooses to add a picture as the app’s artwork in the Picture Studio. The image is stored on the device for use inside the app only.
  • Location — not used. The app does not request or use your location. (A system message about location may appear only because of a standard file-picker component; the app never accesses location.)

How the app connects to the internet

The app works offline. The only network use is downloading display fonts from Google Fonts the first time they are needed, and the device’s own built-in text-to-speech voice for reading words aloud. These do not involve any personal data about you or your child.

Children’s privacy

HNL Learning is designed for children and complies with the principle of collecting no personal data from anyone, including children (consistent with COPPA and GDPR-K). Because we collect nothing, there is nothing about your child to access, share, or sell. A simple parent gate guards the settings area.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
