# HNL Learning — Story scripts + picture prompts

Use this to make illustrations for each story scene, then upload them in the app.

## How to use
1. **First, paste the "Cast reference" block once** into ChatGPT so every picture keeps the same characters.
2. Then paste a scene's **🖼️ Image prompt** to generate that picture (one square image per scene, **no text in the image**).
3. In the app: **Settings (gear, code 1‑2‑3‑4) → Picture Studio → 📖 _story name_**, and upload each picture in order (Picture 1 = scene 1, etc.).

**Style to keep:** soft flat cartoon **storybook illustration for kids 3–8**, bright cheerful colors, warm Somali **savanna** (acacia trees, golden sand, blue sky, sun), friendly and gentle (never scary), **square (1:1), no words/letters in the picture**.

## 🎭 Cast reference (paste this first)
> Keep these characters consistent in every illustration. Soft flat cartoon storybook style for young kids, square, no text:
> - **Libaax (lion):** friendly cartoon lion, golden-yellow fur, big fluffy orange mane, round cute eyes; big white teeth show only when roaring.
> - **Dawaco (fox):** clever cartoon fox, bright orange fur, white muzzle and chest, **big bushy orange tail with a cream tip**, pointy ears with dark tips.
> - **Jiir (mouse):** tiny grey cartoon mouse, **big round ears with pink inside**, pink nose, very cute.
> - **Geel (camel):** cartoon dromedary camel, sandy-tan, **one hump**, long neck, goofy friendly face with long eyelashes.
> - **Waraabe (hyena):** cartoon spotted hyena, sandy-grey with brown spots, big round ears, sloping back, goofy toothy grin (friendly, not scary).
> - **Wiil Waal (boy):** clever Somali boy, brown skin, short dark hair, **round glasses, holding a red book**.
> - **Dhegdheer (ogre woman):** FUNNY not scary cartoon ogre, greenish skin, **ONE enormous ear** on one side, wild dark hair, big round eyes, goofy grin; child-friendly.
> - **Carruurta (children):** a young Somali boy and girl, simple cute cartoon style.
> - **Odayga (elder) / Ninka Xikmadda (wise man):** kind old Somali man, white beard, simple robe.
> - **Ari (goat):** cute cream/white goat, small curved horns, little beard, floppy ears.
> - **Shimbir (bird):** tiny bright blue bird with a yellow belly and orange beak, cheerful.
> - **Boqor (king):** friendly Somali king, brown skin, white beard, **gold crown**, maroon royal robe with a gold sash.

**3.** Libaax wuu qoslay oo wuxuu muujiyey ilkihiisa waaweyn. Si kibir ah ayuu u tegey — godka qotodheer ee hortiisana ma uu arkin. Dhac! Hoos ayuu u dhacay!

**4.** Libaax wuu ciyey oo derbiyada ku xardhay, laakiin wuu awoodi waayey inuu kor u baxo. Xooggiisa weyni kuma filnayn.

**5.** Dawaco way maqashay. Way fikirtay oo fikirtay. Markaas waxay godka ku soo giringirisay jirid weyn, sidii sallaan Libaax ku fuulo.

**6.** Libaax wuu kor u baxay, ugu dambeyntiina nabad galay. Si tartiib ah ayuu Dawaco ugu mahadceliyey, labadooduna waxay noqdeen saaxiibo aad isu jecel.
